Info

  • Use the search box to filter by Pokémon name or moves.
  • Click a role chip to filter by role, or click it again to remove that filter.
  • Click a Pokémon image or name for the build popup, a move image for the move popup, and the Win Rate value for the row popup.
  • Click the table headers to sort, or use the mobile Sort panel on smaller screens.
  • Hide Tiers switches the table to Win Rate sorting and hides the Tier column.

Tier

  • Tier is an estimate, not a perfect ranking.
  • Win rate is the main signal, pick rate helps show whether the data is trustworthy, and ban rate nudges the result a little.
  • Very strong outliers become S, the middle of the list uses A through D, and the weakest rows fall to F.
  • Rows below the default trust cutoff can stay hidden until you turn them on or adjust filters.
