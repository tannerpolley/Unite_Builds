Info

  • Tap Pokemon for passive, attack, Unite Move, and patches
  • Tap moves for descriptions and move patch history
  • Tier is a sample-damped composite of win rate, pick rate, and Pokémon ban rate
  • Raw score = 0.72 * z(win rate) + 0.14 * z(pick rate) + 0.08 * z(ban rate) + 0.07 * z(win rate) * z(pick rate) + 0.02 * z(win rate) * z(ban rate) + 0.01 * z(pick rate) * z(ban rate)
  • Sample confidence = 1 - exp(-estimated picks / 1200)
  • Damped score = raw score * (0.25 + (1 - 0.25) * sample confidence)
  • Tier Score = z(damped score)
  • Labels = S+ at 3.25, S at 0.75, A at 0.00, B at -0.50, C at -0.75, and D below that
  • Display cutoff = pick rate at or above 0.5%
  • Tap Win Rate for build, battle item, and held item details
  • Use Sort and Filters to refine the list
  • Swipe and scroll within popups to explore details on mobile
